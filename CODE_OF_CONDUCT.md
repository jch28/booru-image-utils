# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone in our community.

## Standards

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct may be reported to the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

For more details on community standards, visit [rule34.ink/community](https://rule34.ink/ref/anvil?to=CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
